January Meetup with Speaker Don Ankney

2023-01-05 :: Julie
#meetup  #techtalk 

When: Sunday, January 15, 2023, 1:30pm

Where: University Place Library Meeting Room at 3609 Market Pl W, University Place, WA 98466.

Join South Sound Hackerspace for our second all ages meetup! Don Ankney will do a talk titled, “How to Get a Job” that he put together while teaching at UW Seattle. Join others in the Tacoma area tech community, and those trying to break into the industry, to learn and network.

Don Ankney has spent 20 years in the security industry, mostly at large tech companies. He has been a security researcher at Microsoft, served as head of engineering for Apple Product Security, and currently holds his dream job – leading a team focused on deep security research in Open Source at AWS.
